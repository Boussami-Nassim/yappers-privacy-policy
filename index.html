1. Introduction

Life Lens ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Yappers mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

Yappers is an anonymous, real-time public feed where users can share text messages, GIFs, and trigger animations visible to all connected users. The App is designed to be used without any login or account registration.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Yappers is built around anonymity and minimal data collection. We do not require registration, login, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information to use the App.

2.1 Information You Provide

Username
When you first open the App, a random anonymous username is automatically generated (e.g., "Anon_3847"). You can also set a custom username of up to 30 characters. Your username is stored locally on your device and is not linked to your identity. It is sent with each message so other users can see who posted.
Messages
Any text messages you submit are broadcast to all connected users in real time. Messages are stored on our servers for feed history purposes, allowing users to view recent messages when they open the App.
Reply Messages
Users can reply to other users' messages. Replies are broadcast to all connected users in real time, the same as regular messages. Reply messages reference the original message being replied to.
Reactions
Users can react to messages with emojis (👍 ❤️ 😂 🔥 😮). Reactions are broadcast to all connected users in real time. No personal data is associated with reactions beyond your anonymous username.
GIFs
When you send a GIF, the GIF metadata (URL, dimensions, and content ID from our third-party GIF provider) is broadcast to all connected users. We do not store GIF selections.
Animations
When you trigger an animation (e.g., confetti), the animation name is broadcast to all connected users. No personal data is associated with this action.
Coin Balance
Users earn and spend virtual coins within the App. The coin balance is stored locally on your device via DataStore. It is never transmitted to our servers.

2.2 Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be explicitly clear about what we do not collect:

Real names Email addresses Phone numbers Physical addresses Payment information Photos or media Contacts Location data Biometric data

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

The Google AdMob SDK, which we use to display rewarded video advertisements, may automatically collect device information, advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID), and ad interaction data for the purpose of serving personalized ads. For details on what data AdMob collects and how it is used, please refer to Google's advertising privacy policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's ad settings.

Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM), which we use for push notifications, collects a device token for notification delivery. This token is used solely to route notifications to your device and is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Your IP address may be visible to our hosting infrastructure as part of standard internet communication (WebSocket and HTTP connections), but we do not log, store, or process IP addresses for any purpose.

3. How We Use Information

The information we handle is used for the following purposes:

Message Delivery
To broadcast your text messages, replies, reactions, GIFs, and animation triggers to all connected users in real time via WebSocket.
Content Moderation
We apply a server-side filter to block messages containing hate speech, racist slurs, and other harmful content that violates community standards. Messages that trigger this filter are rejected and never broadcast. This filtering is done in real time and no rejected message content is stored.
Explicit Language Filtering
The App includes an optional client-side explicit language filter. When enabled, profane or explicit words are replaced with asterisks on your device before being displayed. This filtering happens entirely on your device — no data about your filter preference is sent to our servers.
Push Notifications
We use Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver push notifications about new activity in the feed. Users can disable notifications at any time in their device settings. We store a last-notification timestamp locally on your device to implement a cooldown period (currently 5 minutes) to avoid excessive notifications.
Advertising
We display rewarded video ads via Google AdMob. Users can choose to watch an ad to earn virtual coins (10 coins per ad, once per day). AdMob may use device identifiers and ad interaction data to serve ads. See Google's advertising privacy policy for details.
In-App Purchases
Users can purchase virtual coin packages through Google Play Billing. Purchase transactions are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not collect, store, or have access to payment information (credit card numbers, billing addresses, etc.). We only receive confirmation of successful purchases to credit the corresponding coin amount.
Virtual Currency
Coins are used to unlock premium animations and text effects within the App. Coin balance is tracked locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

4. Data Storage & Retention

Messages submitted through the App are stored on our servers for feed history purposes, allowing users to view recent messages when they open the App. Messages are associated only with your anonymous username and do not contain personally identifiable information.

The following data is stored locally on your device only (never transmitted to our servers except where noted): your anonymous username (sent with each message so other users can see who posted), your theme preference (dark or light mode), your explicit language filter preference (on or off), your onboarding completion status, a cached word list for the client-side explicit language filter, your coin balance, effect usage tracking (date and counts for daily usage limits), last daily bonus claim date, last rewarded ad watched date, terms acceptance status, and last notification timestamp.

This local data can be cleared at any time by clearing the App's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

Klipy (GIF Provider)
When you use the GIF picker, the App communicates directly with Klipy's API (klipy.com) to search and retrieve GIFs. Klipy may collect certain information as described in their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review Klipy's privacy policy at klipy.com/support/privacy-policy. We do not share any personal information with Klipy — GIF search queries are the only data transmitted to their service.
Google AdMob (Advertising)
We use Google AdMob to display rewarded video advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, ad interaction data, and other information as described in Google's privacy policy. Users can opt out of personalized advertising through their device's ad settings.
Google Play Billing (In-App Purchases)
In-app purchases are processed through Google Play. Google handles all payment processing. We do not receive or store any payment details such as credit card numbers or billing addresses. Google's privacy policy applies to purchase transactions.
Firebase Cloud Messaging (Push Notifications)
We use FCM to send push notifications about new activity in the feed. FCM assigns a device token for notification delivery. This token is not linked to any personally identifiable information. Google's privacy policy applies.

6. Children's Privacy

Yappers is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since the App does not require registration and does not collect personal data, there is minimal risk of inadvertently collecting children's information. However, the App contains a public feed with user-generated content that may include explicit language (when the explicit filter is disabled), and is therefore intended for users aged 13 and older.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us at the email address below and we will take steps to address the concern.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information handled by the App. Communication between the App and our servers uses encrypted connections (WSS/HTTPS). Since we collect minimal user data and do not store personally identifiable information, the risk of a data breach affecting personal information is minimal.

Important: Any message you send through the App is broadcast to all connected users in real time. Do not share personal, sensitive, or confidential information in the public feed, as it will be visible to anyone currently using the App.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or process personally identifiable data on our servers, traditional data subject rights (access, correction, deletion, portability) are not directly applicable to our service. However:

Local data: You can delete all locally stored data at any time by clearing the App's storage in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

Messages: Messages are stored on our servers for feed history purposes and are associated only with your anonymous username. Since messages do not contain personally identifiable information, individual message deletion requests are not supported.

Ad personalization: You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings (Settings → Google → Ads on Android devices).

In-app purchases: In-app purchases of virtual coins are processed by Google Play and are subject to Google Play's refund policy. We do not process refunds directly.

9. Content Moderation

To maintain a safe environment, the App employs the following content moderation measures:

Server-side hard filter: Messages containing hate speech, racist terminology, white supremacist content, and similar harmful material are automatically blocked and never broadcast to users.

Client-side explicit filter: Users can enable an optional filter in Settings that redacts profane and explicit language with asterisks. This is a display preference that operates entirely on your device.

We do not employ human moderators and do not review individual messages. The content filtering is entirely automated.

10. International Users

Yappers is available to users worldwide. Our servers may be located in different regions. By using the App, you acknowledge that data involved in message delivery and storage may be processed in a country other than your own. Since we do not store personally identifiable information, cross-border data transfer concerns are minimal.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

For significant changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify users through the App or other appropriate channels.
