Privacy Policy

App: Yappers
Developer: Life Lens
Effective Date: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Life Lens ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Yappers mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

Yappers is an anonymous, real-time public feed where users can share text messages, GIFs, and trigger animations visible to all connected users. The App is designed to be used without any login or account registration.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Yappers is built around anonymity and minimal data collection. We do not require registration, login, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information to use the App.

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Username: When you first open the App, a random anonymous username is automatically generated (e.g., "Anon_3847"). This is stored locally on your device and is not linked to your identity.
  • Messages: Text messages are broadcast in real time. They are not stored on our servers and are discarded after transmission.
  • GIFs: Metadata (URL, dimensions) from our provider is broadcast. We do not store your selections.
  • Animations: Animation triggers are broadcast without personal data association.

2.2 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect: real names, emails, phone numbers, physical addresses, financial info, photos, contacts, location data, or biometric data.

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

The App does not use analytics SDKs or advertising trackers. Your IP address may be visible to our hosting infrastructure during communication, but we do not log, store, or process IP addresses for any profiling purposes.

3. How We Use Information

  • Message delivery: To broadcast content via WebSocket.
  • Content moderation: Automated server-side filters block hate speech and harmful content in real time.
  • Explicit language filtering: Optional client-side filtering happens entirely on your device.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Yappers operates on a "fire-and-forget" model. We maintain no message database or chat history. Data like your username and app preferences stay locally on your device and can be cleared via your device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Klipy (GIF Provider): The App communicates with Klipy’s API for GIF searches. Klipy may collect information as per their privacy policy. No personal info is shared with them by us.

6. Children’s Privacy

Yappers is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Data Security

We use encrypted connections (WSS/HTTPS). However, remember that any message you send is public and visible to all active users. Do not share sensitive information.

8. Your Rights

Because we do not store personal data on our servers, traditional data access/deletion requests are handled by the user clearing the App’s storage or uninstalling the App.

9. Content Moderation

We use automated server-side hard filters for harmful content and optional client-side display filters for profanity. No human moderation is employed.
